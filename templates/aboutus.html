About Show Time

Welcome to Show Time 🎥 This is a simple and user-friendly movie ticket booking system website. Here users can view available movies, select show timings, choose seats, and book tickets easily.

✨ Our Mission

Our mission is to make movie ticket booking simple, fast, and easy for everyone. We provide an online platform where users can book tickets anytime.

🎟️ What We Offer

  • Latest Movies Information
  • Show Time Selection
  • Seat Selection
  • Instant Ticket Booking
  • User Friendly Design

⭐ Why Choose Us?

Show Time is designed for beginners and students to understand how a movie booking system works using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
